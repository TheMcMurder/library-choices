Hours of Operation
Open 44 hours a week, 10 of those hours are outside of the normal 9-5 working hours. Hours open could be changed or lessened but that impacts the accessibility of the library.
I like to have 2 staff members working at a time Monday- Friday. For safety, planning programming and displays, and to handle the busy times. It is even nice to have 3 workers when planning or implementing programs.
With this type of schedule, we need 84 work hours for staff a week. Adult programming mostly happens when we are closed to accommodate events which means staff is needed to fill those extra hours too.  
Clerks were making $20/hr now they are making $12/hr
Volunteers could be used but I would not use them to staff hours open. They could be the second or third person but ideally you would always have a paid staff member anytime the library is open.

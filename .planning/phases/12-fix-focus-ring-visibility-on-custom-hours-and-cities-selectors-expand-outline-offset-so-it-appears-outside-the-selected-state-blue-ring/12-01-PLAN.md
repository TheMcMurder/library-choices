<objective>
Fix focus ring visibility on staffing and city card selectors so the keyboard focus indicator appears outside the selected-state blue ring, not hidden behind it.

Purpose: When a card is both selected (has-[:checked]:ring-2 ring-blue-600) and focused, the current focus indicator (has-[:focus-visible]:ring-2 ring-blue-600) is identical to and overlaps the selection ring, making it invisible. Switching from ring to outline with outline-offset places the focus indicator outside the selection ring.

Output: Updated src/index.html with outline-based focus indicators on both staffing and city card labels.
</objective>

On both the staffing card label (the `<label for="staffing-{{ level.id }}">` element, around line 26) and the city card label (the `<label for="city-{{ city.id }}">` element, around line 102), replace the focus-visible ring classes with outline classes:

REMOVE these two classes from each label:
  has-[:focus-visible]:ring-2 has-[:focus-visible]:ring-blue-600

ADD these four classes to each label in their place:
  has-[:focus-visible]:outline has-[:focus-visible]:outline-2 has-[:focus-visible]:outline-blue-600 has-[:focus-visible]:outline-offset-4

The resulting class strings should be:

For staffing labels:
```
class="block cursor-pointer rounded-lg border p-6 transition-colors
       has-[:checked]:bg-blue-50 has-[:checked]:ring-2 has-[:checked]:ring-blue-600
       bg-gray-50 border-gray-200
```

For city labels:
```
class="block cursor-pointer rounded-lg border p-6 transition-colors
       has-[:checked]:bg-blue-50 has-[:checked]:ring-2 has-[:checked]:ring-blue-600
       bg-gray-50 border-gray-200
       has-[:focus-visible]:outline has-[:focus-visible]:outline-2 has-[:focus-visible]:outline-blue-600 has-[:focus-visible]:outline-offset-4
       min-h-[44px]"
```

Why outline-offset-4 (4px): The selection state uses ring-2 which renders as a 2px box-shadow. outline-offset-4 pushes the focus outline 4px from the border edge, clearing the ring entirely and providing visible separation.
